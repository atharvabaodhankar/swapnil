About Me

I'm Swapnil Javanjal — a Senior Vice President of Software Development at JPMorgan Chase & Co., a technology leader, and a passionate innovator in distributed systems and backend development. With over nine years of experience spanning banking, tech, finance, and healthcare, I've built robust, scalable, and secure applications that drive business transformation and enhance customer satisfaction.

My expertise lies in designing and developing cutting-edge solutions using a wide array of technologies — Java, Python, C#, React, Kafka, Docker, AWS, Spring Boot, and more. I thrive in fast-paced environments where complex problems need creative, efficient solutions.

Throughout my career, I've led high-impact projects like the development of JPMorgan's Checkout product, spearheaded the launch of transformative payment solutions, and driven architectural decisions that have shaped the future of digital payments.

Beyond my professional role, I'm an AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner, a Global Hackathon 2022 winner, and a contributor to the International Journal of Scientific Engineering and Research. I believe in continuous learning, sharing knowledge, and pushing the boundaries of innovation.
